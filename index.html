From first lead to long-term care

Every project moves through the same eight stages — each with an explicit gate it has to clear before it advances. Design, dev and QA loop in practice; the hub always shows where things really stand.

    Lead
    Qualify and capture intake
  2. 02

    Scope, sitemap, sign-off
  3. 03

    Signed deal, deposit in
  4. 04
    Design
    Wireframe to approved hi-fi
  5. 05
    Development
    Build, branches, staging URL
  6. 06
    QA / staging
    Test, fix, client UAT
  7. 07

    Go live and verified
  8. 08
    Maintenance
    Retainer, monitor, support

Drag a project forward and the gate checklist appears — nothing slips a stage by accident.
